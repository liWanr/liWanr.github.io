# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

To build a friendly and open community for this project, all participants are expected to follow these rules.  
为了建设一个友好、开放的社区，本项目的所有参与者应遵守以下规则。

- Be respectful and polite in all discussions.  
  在所有讨论中保持尊重和礼貌。
- Do not engage in personal attacks, harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.  
  禁止人身攻击、骚扰、仇恨言论或任何形式的歧视。
- Focus on the topic and technical issues, avoid heated or off-topic discussions.  
  讨论应聚焦于主题和技术问题，避免引战或跑题。
- Do not publish illegal content or content that infringes others' copyrights or privacy.  
  禁止发布违法内容，以及侵犯他人版权或隐私的内容。
- Assume good faith, and try to communicate calmly when disagreements occur.  
  在出现分歧时，尽量保持冷静沟通，并假设对方是善意的。

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and email communications.  
本行为准则适用于本项目的所有空间，包括 issue、PR、讨论区及邮件往来。

Project maintainers may edit, remove content, or restrict participation of users who seriously violate this Code of Conduct.  
项目维护者有权在必要时编辑或删除不当内容，并限制严重违反本行为准则的用户参与项目。

如对本行为准则有任何疑问，或需要举报不当行为，请联系：
